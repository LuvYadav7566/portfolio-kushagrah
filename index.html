About Me

I’m Kushagrah Yadav, a pharmacy student and researcher who loves understanding how medicines work inside the body. I enjoy reading research papers, learning about formulations, and exploring how new drugs can make life better and safer for people.

When I’m not in the lab or studying, you’ll probably find me on my bike. Long rides, open roads, and beautiful views help me relax and think clearly. I also like creating simple, clear content about pharmacy, bikes, and daily life to share my learning with others.

I believe in safe driving, discipline and consistency. Whether it’s on the road, in the lab, or in front of a camera, I always try to stay honest, focused, and curious.
